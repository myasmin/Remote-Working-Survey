Dashboard for Remote Working Survey Analysis

Introduction

Welcome to the dashboard for the Remote Working Survey analysis. This survey, conducted by NSW Treasury and published by Maven Analytics, explores participants' perspectives on remote, onsite, and hybrid working environments during the pandemic.

Access the full analysis on GitHub: Remote Working Survey Analysis.

Highlights

  • Comparison of Remote, Onsite, and Hybrid work environments.
  • Insights into demographic and professional characteristics of participants.
  • Data visualizations of participants' preferences and time management expectations.
